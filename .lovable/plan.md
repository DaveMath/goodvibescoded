
# Good Vibes Coded — 1970s Retro Landing Page

## Overview
A single-page, groovy 1970s-themed landing page for **goodvibescoded.com** featuring a starburst sun design, retro color palette (yellows, oranges, warm browns), and playful typography.

## Visual Design
- **Color palette**: Warm 70s tones — golden yellows, burnt oranges, warm browns, and cream backgrounds
- **Starburst rays**: Radiating sunburst pattern filling the background, classic 70s style
- **Font**: Bubble/balloon-style display font (Google Font like "Bungee Shade" or "Fredoka One") for the main text

## Layout & Elements

### Center Sun
- A large circular sun graphic in the center of the page with a warm yellow-to-orange gradient
- Surrounded by retro starburst rays extending outward

### "Good Vibes Coded" Arc Text
- The words **"Good Vibes Coded"** arranged in a half-circle arc above the sun
- Styled in a bubbly/balloon font with a retro feel

### Rotating Funny Words
- Below/over the sun, a series of humorous "vibe coding" parody words fade in and out one at a time
- Words like: "Cheap", "Wrongly", "Insecure", "Recklessly", "Hastily", "Blindly", "Chaotically", "Duct-Taped", "Yolo'd", "Unreviewed", "Hallucinated"
- Smooth fade-in/fade-out animation cycling through the list

### Footer
- **"Copyright 2026 — A DaveMathews.com creation."**
- "DaveMathews.com" is a clickable link pointing to `https://davemathews.com`
- Styled in a smaller retro font, warm-toned to match the theme
